  • SELBYME Return & Refund Policy

    At SELBYME, we strive to deliver the best quality products to our customers. However, if you receive an expired product or a different item than what you ordered, we offer a return and refund option under the following conditions:

    1. Eligible Return & Refund Cases

    • The product is expired at the time of delivery.
    • The product is different from what was ordered (wrong item delivered).

    2. Return & Refund Timeframe

    You must request a return within 2 hours of receiving the delivery.

    3. Return Process

    • Contact our customer support via WhatsApp at WhatsApp Link or call us.
    • Provide your order details and clear images of the received product.
    • Once verified, we will arrange for a return pickup or guide you on the return process.

    4. Refund Method

    • Refunds will be processed within 24 hours after return verification.
    • Refunds will be issued as store credit or original payment method (where applicable).

    5. Non-Returnable Items

    • Products that are not expired or not incorrect as per the order.
    • Requests made after the 2-hour return window.
    • Opened or used products unless they were delivered expired.

    6. Important Notes

    • Customers must check the products at the time of delivery.
    • For cash-on-delivery orders, refunds will be provided via UPI or store credit.
    • SELBYME reserves the right to reject return requests that do not meet the policy criteria.
