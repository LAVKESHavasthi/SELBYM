About SELBYME

Welcome to SELBYME — a new-age platform revolutionizing local shopping and delivery across India! SELBYME was founded with the mission to connect customers directly with nearby shops and deliver products quickly at the best prices. We aim to make everyday shopping easier, faster, and more affordable for everyone.

At SELBYME, we blend technology with tradition, bringing together the trust of local stores and the convenience of online shopping. Whether it's groceries, vegetables, milk, or daily essentials — SELBYME ensures doorstep delivery with love and care.
